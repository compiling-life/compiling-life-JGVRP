Questions & Answers

What does it mean when Peacetime is Disabled?

➞ All activities that break normal peacetime such as Running lights, Drifting, ETC is allowed. If you have priority, you may evade LEO on foot and/or in a vehicle. Civilians are allowed to be on the Criminal team.

What does it mean when Peacetime is Strict?

➞ Breaking any in-game laws are strictly prohibited and will lead to a double infraction. Strict Peacetime is usually used as a session reset if it gets too bad.

What does it mean when Peacetime is Normal?

➞ Activities that would break peacetime such as Drifting, Committing crimes, Evading LEO, ETC are not allowed and will lead to a infraction. Being on the criminal team is not allowed.

What will I get if I boost once?

➞ Server Booster Role, Slotted Vehicle Exempt, & 100k Economy Money.

What will I get if I boost twice?

➞ Same perks as above, Banned Vehicle Exempt, & 150k Economy Money.

What will I get if I boost 3 times?

➞ Same perks as above, Blacklisted Vehicle Exempt, Vehicle Registration Exempt, & Server Contributor Role.

How can I get vehicle exemptions without boosting and/or with robux?

➞ You can obtain them by heading to the roblox group, which can be found on the links page, and buy shirts with robux. Afterwards, you may make a support ticket to claim your roles.

How can I coordinate a free partnership?

➞ You must have 7500 members, active staff, daily sessions, ensure that your server is roblox oriented, and that it has a good reputation. If your server meets all of these requirements, you may make a support ticket to claim a partnership.

How can I coordinate a paid partnership?

➞ If you are interested in advertising your server with an "@here" ping, it will cost you 2500 robux. For the "@everyone" ping, it will cost you 4500 robux. If you are interested, you can head to the roblox group which can be found in the links section and make a support ticket.

How do I register/unregister my vehicle?

➞ First, you must head to the #economy or #bot-commands channel, and run either the /register or /unregister command. Once the command pulls up, complete the following tasks and fill out the boxes and it should work perfectly.

How can I get insurance or handicap parking?

➞ First, you must head to either the #economy or #bot-commands channel, and you can run the /store command. To buy it, you just use the /buy command and then once bought, you must use the /use command.

How many subscribers/followers do I need in order to obtain the Content Creator Role?

➞ For Tiktok, you must have atleast 1.5k followers. For Youtube, you must have atleast 2.5k subscribers. If you meet these requirements, open a support ticket to gain your roles.

What are the requirements to become a Server Contributor & To Obtain Its Role?

➞ You must either have exclusive member or atleast have boosted the server twice & obtain 5k+ messages.

How do I get a license?

➞ You can get a license by heading to the #license-request channel under the roleplay engagement section. Once there, just fill out the format and somebody will give you the license.

How long do applications take to be read?

➞ Applications dont have a fixed read time, they are read whenever administrators are available to read them and the average time it takes is up to a week depending on the opportunity you applied to.

What happens when you get your ban appealed?

➞ All of your previous logs will be removed and in order to join any opportunities, you must either re-apply/fastpass.

How can I find all of the blacklisted servers?

➞ Normally, you could scroll through all of the server-adjustments, which can be tiring. An easy way to view all of the blacklisted servers is by heading to the #staff-fastpasses channel and viewing all of the blacklisted servers there.

How can I find what vehicles are restricted?

➞ In order to figure out which vehicles are restricted, you can head to the #civilian-information channel or the links page here and figure out if your vehicle is restricted by heading to the restricted vehicle's guide. You can also search up certain vehicle attributes with the Cmd+F or Ctrl+F key on your keyboard.
