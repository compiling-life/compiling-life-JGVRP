Guidelines

  • ➞ You must be 13+ to be in this roleplay community. If we are informed that you're younger than 13, you will automatically be banned from JGVRP until your of age.
  • ➞ NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content is strictly prohibited within JGVRP. Sending NSFW content within any channel will lead to administrative punishments.
  • ➞ Inappropriate Language is allowed to an extent but using vulgar language and/or profanity towards others within the community will allow you to receive an administrative punishment.
  • ➞ Any discrimination against some ones; Race, Sexuality, Belief's, etc will lead to a automatic ban. Even if meant as a joke, punishments will still be placed.
  • ➞ Having alternate accounts within JGVRP are strictly prohibited. Using alternate accounts to avoid punishments, ETC will result to a automatic ban.
  • ➞ Any self, or server advertising relating to any other individual server not related to JoJe's Greenville Roleplay will lead to an automatic ban, this includes, but is not limited to DM Advertising, Spamming Invites, or even persuading users to join your server.
  • ➞ Personal Information is suggested to not be shared amongst people within Discord, this is due to several privacy issues. Leaking any information that is provided to you from other members will lead to an automatic ban. This includes, but is not limited to Phone Numbers, Home / Work Addresses, etc.
  • ➞ Speaking about, or pertaining to any delicate matters which may include Self Harm, Sexual Harassment, 9 / 11, Sexism, etc will lead to administrative punishments. This server is mean to be kept a safe and calm space.
  • ➞ All information posted within JoJe's Greenville Roleplay has been created, or updated by former, or current High Command members. Stealing any information provided within the server will lead to an automatic ban + blacklist from the server. This includes but is not limited to Applications, Server Templates, Server Documents, etc.
  • ➞ Asking staff members to host is strictly prohibited and will lead to an administrative punishment as staff members will host when they are available and have time. A timeout will also be applied. This includes but is not limited to; "session?", "when will the next session be", etc.
  • ➞ Civilian Disrespect will not be tolerated. Disrespecting another civilian and/or any other user in the server will get you an administrative punishment. This includes but is not limited to; Using profanity against one another, Being vaguely rude, etc.
  • ➞ If anyone breaks Discord Terms of Service or fails to abide by their Community Guidelines, you will be banned from the community and not let back in unless deemed false. We moderate Discord TOS and Discord Community Guidelines heavily and breaking any of those regulations is not allowed.
  • ➞ JoJe's Greenville Roleplay does not condone anyone breaking Discord Terms of Service or Discords Community Guidelines. Therefore, anything outside of JGVRP will not be moderated with Discord TOS being an exception.
