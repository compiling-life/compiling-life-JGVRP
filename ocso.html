Outagamie County Sheriff's Office (OCSO)

What It’s About

The Outagamie County Sheriff's Office. Our mission is to work in partnership with the community and other public service agencies to protect and serve the citizens of Outagamie County with P.R.I.D.E., which stands for Professionalism, Respect, Integrity, Dedication, and Excellence.

The Sheriff's Office provides various services, including law enforcement, National Park Service, Fox Valley Metro Police Department. They are dedicated to maintaining a safe environment for the community and ensuring fair and equal treatment for everyone.

National Park Services

Our mission is to always keep our parks protected & our visitors safe and to make sure all of our rules are being enforced. We are very committed to keeping our park a natural beauty and to preserve a safe and enjoyable time for everybody.

Fox Valley Metro Police Department

The Fox Valley Metro Police enforce laws, assist motorists, inspect vehicles, and support local agencies in emergencies. They uphold the highest standards set by the community and command.
